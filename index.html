Draught Proofing

As well as being re-corded and fully serviced as below,  your sash windows will glide beautifully on hidden brushes fitted in new traditional high quality timber beadings that will not detract from the look of your historic windows.

Our top quality procedure:

  • Eliminates draughts and rattles
  • Saves heat and energy bills
  • Significantly reduces external noise

Full Service with Re-Cording

We will get your windows working beautifully again. Broken cords, seized pulleys, jammed and painted-over windows or unbalanced weights can often stop your windows opening and closing as they would have when they were originally installed.

Rot Repairs

We can carry out all rot repairs to your sash window boxes and sills to fully restore and completely weather proof your windows. This includes top quality stone infills.

Full Restoration of Windows

We will lovingly restore your sashes to their former glory by completely removing your windows and restoring them at our workshop in Portishead. The windows will be fully stripped and any rot repairs carried out with a specialist fully-guaranteed rot repair. This is particularly significant for listed buildings.

Double Glazed Reproduction Windows

If you are looking to improve the thermal performance and greatly reduce noise pollution we produce double-glazed sashes that will fit in your existing sash window frame which reduces overall cost and disruption. All of our new timber double-glazed sashes are finished with a range of traditional sash furniture and installed with the added benefits of our specialist draught-proof system.

Replacement Windows

If your windows are excessively damaged it is often not cost-effective to repair the damage and we would recommend a new high quality fully glazed reproduction window in a choice of soft or hardwoods. This would be fitted with your choice of period or contemporary locks and catches.

Window Furniture & Security Fittings

We supply and fit a large range of superior traditional or contemporary Sash Locks and Fittings to give you full security protection and fully enhance the look of your beautiful sash windows.
